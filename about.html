The Forge?

The Forge is a discord server, a circle of friends who came together to make cool and entertaining mods for the 2D game 'Starbound'.
It specializes in weapons and armor creation, along with other miscellaneous creations.

Previously known as Project Blade Dance's discord server, the server underwent major reforms to get to where it is now.
