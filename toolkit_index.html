A unique assortment of weapons that is capable of healing your allies and hurting your enemies at the same time.

Have you ever thought to youself: "Wow, you can't heal your teammates in the heat of battle in this game"? Probably not, but I've come up with a solution for that problem anyways, this pack adds a new category of weapons to the game, enabling you to become the team's Healer.

Featuring 7 new tools for all of your healing needs:

Nanite Autoinjector.
- Using autoinjector technology, allows nanite to repair damaged tissues and body parts.
Healer's Pistol.
- A pistol with a nanite launcher, this allows you to heal your teammate at range.
Healer's SMG.
- The pistol design was remade into a compact submachine gun, allowing you to dish out even more damage.
Healer's Revolver.
- A high damaging revolver with a slow rate of fire, gives off extra stopping power but you must be very accurate to hit your shots.
Healing Grenade.
- To quote one of our scientists: 'Programmable nanites that will revifiy your allies, while euthanizing those with hostile intent.' We hope that helps.
Healer's Assault Rifle
- The full-blown assault rifle for the healer.
