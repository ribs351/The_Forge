Project Blade Dance: Reloaded

Non-Steam Download

View Project Files

A melee weapon addon for Starbound

Blade Dance was originally released in 2017 as a total overhaul for Starbound's melee weapons. Eventually development ceased due to many bugs that plagued its release, as well as not being a client-sided mod.

Right now it's undergoing a complete rewrite and its main focus (as of this point) is the overhaul of the game's broadswords and shortswords. Main features include:

  • Combos for all swords + variations.
  • 100% client-side. Compatible vanilla multiplayer.
  • Custom crafting recipes and custom crafting stations.
